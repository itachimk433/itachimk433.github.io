1. Introduction

QR Scan & Gen ("the App") is developed by MKDEV. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

The App uses your device camera solely to scan QR codes and barcodes. Scan history is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data on our own servers.

3. Advertising

The App displays advertisements via Huawei Ads (Pangle). This ad network may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with its own privacy policy. MKDEV does not control and is not responsible for data collected by the ad network.

For Huawei Ads privacy information, please visit: consumer.huawei.com/en/privacy

4. Camera Permission

The App requests camera access exclusively to scan QR codes and barcodes. Camera data is processed entirely on-device and is not transmitted externally.

5. Local Storage

Scan history and app preferences (such as settings) are stored locally on your device using Android's standard storage APIs. This data never leaves your device.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
